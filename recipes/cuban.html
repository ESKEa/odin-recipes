Cuban Sandwich

Description

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup mustard
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 loaf Cuban bread
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese
  • 6 thin slices smoked fully-cooked ham
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked pulled pork, heated
  • 1 large dill pickle, sliced lengthwise
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Mix mayo, mustard, and cayenne together to make sauce
  3. Cut bread in half and evenly split bread to make two sandwiches
  4. Spread sauce on both sides
  5. Put 2 slices Swiss cheese, 3 slices ham, cooked pork, pickle slices, and 2 more slices Swiss cheese
  6. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Press down sandwiches with a heavy weight. Toast 3 or 4 minutes per side.
  7. Serve!
