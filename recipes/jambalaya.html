Jambalaya

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons peanut oil
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 10 ounces andouille sausage, sliced
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed Italian tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon file powder
  • 1 1/4 cups uncooked white rice
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of peanut oil in Dutch oven over medium heat. Season the sausage and chicken pieces with Cajun seasoning. Saute sausage until browned. Remove with spoon, set aside.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon peanut oil, and saute chicken pieces until lightly browned on all sides. Set aside.
  3. In the same pot, saute onion, bell pepper, celery, and garlic until tender.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, and season with red pepper, black pepper, salt, hot pepper sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and file powder.
  5. Stir in chicken and sausage. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Stir in the rice and chicken broth.
  7. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes, or until liquid is absorbed.
  8. Serve!
